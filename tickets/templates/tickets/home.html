Welcome to MothRadar

Submit tickets.

MothRadar is the issue tracker for our UnicornAttractor app.

While the UnicornAttractor is awesome in every way, we are aware that it is not immune to bugs. Therefore we provide you with MothRadar to raise a ticket on any Bug you find, so we can throw the bug out at the first opportunity.

You know a way to make our UnicornAttractor even more awesome?
In that case, you can raise a ticket on a Feature that you would like us to implement in the app.

Browse existing issues.

Being a superb radar, MothRadar never loses track of any bug that enters into its sight.

To make it easier for you to find a specific Bug or Feature ticket, use the Search functionality in the Tickets view.

Or, if you prefer to browse, use one of the awesome filters we've created for you, to view Bugs only or Features only.

You can even filter your tickets only just to remind yourself how awesome you are at bug-spotting.

Upvote tickets to get them fixed sooner.

While keeping UnicornAttractor bug-free is our top priority, it is impossible to attend to all bugs and features simultaneously. That is why we have implemented our ticket upvoting system.

Just find that Bug you really really want to get rid of, and click the Upvote button. That's all there is to it. Upvoting bugs is completely free.

For the Feature tickets, the process is the same, except that upvoting Features is a paid service. Think of it as buying a new shining accessory for your car.

At the end of each month, we will review the upvotes list and dedicate 50% of our maintenance work time to fixing Bugs (with priority given to the top-voted ones), and the other 50% to implementing the most upvoted Feature on the list.

Comment on issues to help others.

You have found a way around a bug? Please let our users (and us!) know about it by leaving a comment below the ticket.

Perhaps you have additional information (or a different viewpoint) on a bug already described? Great, put it in a comment, it will help everyone!

You can even just debate if something is a bug or a feature - we take all user comments seriously.

We have just one requirement: please keep the discussion civilised at all times.
